The New PSA

A system that will deliver real-time safety information relevant to you via SMS.
We use real-time 911 call data to find out any safety issues in your area.
Cutting out the middleman, you are able to know relevent information before its too late.

Twilio Integration

Using Twilio's API, we are able to delivery you time-sensitive information at a price local governments can afford. With SMS service, we can assure the entire community has access to alerts.

Google App
Engine

For reliability, we have implemented Google App Engine to host our services, so that you always get important information.

Socrata Data

Socrata's API has allowed us to get timely information from Seattle's 911 call centres.
